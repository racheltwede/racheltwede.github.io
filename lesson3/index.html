Site Name

The Weather Corner: Weather and news for the communities of Idaho's southeast corner

Site Purpose

The Weather Corner's mission is threefold. First, we aim to provide the residents of Idaho's southeastern corner with information about local weather and upcoming cultural events. Second, we aim to promote a sense of pride and identity for these residents by providing a space online dedicated to their home area, including upcoming events and town history. Third, by making this information available to the public, we hope to make planning for visitors easier and more convenient.
